Hey there, I’m Anderson, a UI/UX designer with over 4 years of experience creating user interfaces and user experiences for digital products and services.

 

Based in Araçatuba, I’ve worked for small technology startups to medium enterprises. Currently, I’m a web designer at Clique Mídias, where I focus on designing websites and building them with no/low-code platforms, like WordPress.

 

I bring a diverse set of both soft and hard skills to the table, ranging from developing UI/UX layouts and creative problem-solving to information design. Proficient in various design tools, I am well-equipped to tackle any design challenge.
